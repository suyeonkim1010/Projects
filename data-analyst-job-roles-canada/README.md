# Data Analyst Job Roles in Canada

Exploratory analysis of 1,700+ Canadian Data Analyst job postings sourced from public job boards (Indeed & Glassdoor).

This project analyzes job demand and salary patterns across provinces to demonstrate practical data cleaning, analysis, and dashboarding skills relevant to Data Analyst and BI roles.

---

## Key Insights
- Ontario accounts for the highest number of Data Analyst job postings in Canada
- Average annual salary is approximately **$78,000 CAD**
- Senior and Systems/Data Analyst roles dominate overall demand
- Job postings are highly concentrated in a small number of provinces
